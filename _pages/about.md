## About

I am an Assistant Professor in the [School of Climate Change and Sustainability at Azim Premji University, Bengaluru, India](https://azimpremjiuniversity.edu.in/cccs). My research focuses on physical oceanography and geophysical fluid dynamics, with an emphasis on understanding the multiscale physical processes that regulate ocean circulation, mixing, and climate variability. I also serve as a [Guest Investigator](https://www.whoi.edu/profile/nihar.paul/) at the [Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution (WHOI), Massachusetts, USA](https://www.whoi.edu/).

My research combines satellite observations, *in situ* ocean measurements, theoretical analyses, and high-resolution numerical modeling to investigate equatorial wave dynamics, submesoscale ocean dynamics, internal gravity waves in the ocean, and upper-ocean mixing. I am particularly interested in understanding how interactions across scales influence vertical transport, tracer exchange, energy transfer, and regional climate variability in the Indian Ocean.

Prior to joining [Azim Premji University](https://azimpremjiuniversity.edu.in/), I was a Postdoctoral Investigator at the [Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution, Woods Hole, Massachusetts, USA](https://www.whoi.edu/), where I worked with [Dr. Amala Mahadevan](https://mahadevan.whoi.edu/) and [Dr. J. Thomas Farrar](https://www2.whoi.edu/staff/jfarrar/), both Senior Scientists in the Department of Physical Oceanography and faculty members of the [Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT)](https://www.mit.edu/) - [Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution (WHOI)](https://www.whoi.edu/) Joint Program in Oceanography/Applied Ocean Science and Engineering ([MIT-WHOI Joint Program](https://mit.whoi.edu/)). I have also worked as a postdoctoral researcher with [Dr. Manikandan Mathur](https://ae.iitm.ac.in/aeweb/innerfaculty.php?fname=Manikandan+Mathur), Professor in the [Department of Aerospace Engineering](https://ae.iitm.ac.in/) and [Geophysical Flows Laboratory](https://ae.iitm.ac.in/gfl/) at the [Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai, India](https://www.iitm.ac.in/). I received my Ph.D. from the [Centre for Atmospheric and Oceanic Sciences](https://caos.iisc.ac.in/), [Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru](https://www.iisc.ac.in/), under the supervision of [Dr. Jai Sukhatme](https://caos.iisc.ac.in/jai.html) and [Dr. Debasis Sengupta](https://www.icts.res.in/people/debasis-sengupta).

My long-term research goal is to improve the understanding and prediction of ocean circulation and climate variability by integrating theory, numerical modeling, ocean observations, and emerging data-driven approaches.
